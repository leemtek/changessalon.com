Medi Treatments

Our peels stimulate dramatic changes in the skin. Results vary from mild to deep exfoliation, smoothing, lightening and brightening of acne scars or pigmentation, healing blemishes or pre-mature aging.

Nails

All nail treatments include nail shaping and your choice of either buffing or a beautiful nail color. Each station is equipped with its own sterilization unit and all instruments are sanitized thoroughly before each client’s appointment.
Nail, Hand & Foot Treatments

 

Hair Removal

Waxing is a longer-lasting alternative to shaving for those looking for a hair removal option. The process involves the use of strip wax and hard wax to pull and remove hair from the root. Whether you choose Brazilian waxing, body waxing or expert brow design, you can expect beautiful results.
